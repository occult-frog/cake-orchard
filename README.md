# cake-orchard
Farming game that I'm working on.
Still need to work on art and balancing the game.

Right now you can:
Plant, water, and harvest wheat
Plant, water and harvest sugarcane
Buy Sugarcane Stalks
Buy Salt
Buy Eggs
Buy Vanilla
Buy a Stand Mixer
Make Flour
Make Sugar
Bake a cake

Use 1, 2, and 3 to select inventory slots. To plant, fill up water at the well, or to open the shop or workbench use F. Harvest and water plants using Space. To plant sugarcane buy sugarcane stalks first.

All art is by me. No AI was used in any part of the development process.
